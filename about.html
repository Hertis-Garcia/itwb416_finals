About ITCSpace

ITCSpace is a student-driven project dedicated to sharing resources, announcements, and projects from the College of Information and Computer Studies of De La Salle University - Dasmarinas. This website's aim is to enhance the academic experience of CICS students by providing a centralized platform for information dissemination and collaboration.
