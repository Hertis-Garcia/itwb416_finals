About ITCSpace

ITCSpace is a student-driven project dedicated to sharing resources, announcements, and projects from the College of Information and Computer Studies of De La Salle University - Dasmariñas. This website aims to enhance the academic experience of CICS students by providing a centralized platform for information dissemination and collaboration.

College Profile

The College of Information and Computer Studies (CICS) develops industry-ready technology professionals through rigorous instruction, hands-on projects, and collaboration with industry partners.

Mission, Vision, & Core Values

Vision

To be a leading institution in Information and Computer Studies producing globally competent, ethical, and innovative IT professionals.


Mission

To provide quality IT education that integrates theory and practice, fosters innovation, and encourages collaboration and lifelong learning.


Core Values

  • Excellence
  • Innovation
  • Integrity
  • Collaboration
  • Service

History of the Department

Founded as part of the university’s expansion into technology education, the department has grown into a dynamic academic community with strong partnerships and a legacy of producing capable IT graduates.

Message from the Dean

“At CICS, we believe in empowering students with the skills, values, and mindset needed to thrive in the digital world. Together, we build a community that inspires innovation and leadership.” — Dr. Juan Dela Cruz
