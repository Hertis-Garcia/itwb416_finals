Latest Announcements

The College of Science and Computer Studies Department has officially split itself into two separate Departments. This action follows PAASCU’s recommendation to isolate these departments completely for a more progressive development path.

Overview

De La Salle University - Dasmariñas, with its exceptional quality of education centered in Catholic teaching, provides a place where students can learn and hone their skills in technology. Within the College of Information and Computer Studies (CICS), DLSU-D offers programs in Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) and Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS), both accommodating the different facets of emerging innovations. This prepares graduates to face the real world, enabling them to contribute to the growing needs of society.


Join us as we delve deeper into DLSU-D's CICS, the university's center of technological excellence and solutions.
